Rich Cultural Heritage

Traditional Festivals

Vizag celebrates various traditional festivals like Sankranti, Diwali, and more with grandeur and community participation.

Local Arts and Crafts

Experience the rich local arts and crafts of Vizag, including intricate brass work, traditional paintings, and handloom sarees.

Historical Landmarks

Discover historical landmarks such as the Dutch Cemetery, the ancient Buddhist sites, and the historic King George Hospital.

Savor the Flavors of Vizag

Seafood Delicacies

Vizag is known for its fresh and delicious seafood delicacies, including prawn curry, fish fry, and crab masala.

Andhra Cuisine

Enjoy traditional Andhra cuisine with spicy and flavorful dishes such as biryani, pulusu, and pachadi.

Street Food

Relish the vibrant street food of Vizag, featuring items like murri mixture, bajji, and the famous Vizag masala puri.
